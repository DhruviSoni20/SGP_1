Our Story

We started as a group of like minded people who didn't have many connections but were always curious and eager to be part of everything interesting. This is a student organization at Sweven university dedicated to helping introverted and reserved students to stay informed. We provide all details and news regarding technical, non-technical and cultural events, clubs, and co-curricular activities.
